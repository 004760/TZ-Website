What emotions first come to mind when you eat different color skittles?

Data Collection

We collected data by going around lunch and giving eighth grade students some skittles as well as asking them to take the survey on schoology. We got 138 resoponses!

Visuals

We wanted to use pie charts as visuals to find what people thought about skittles in terms of their color.

Conclusion

Overall green and yellow skittles made people feel sad and depressed as the other colors did not.
We found that When people eat red skittles they first think sweet over savory,happy and sad or depressed.
